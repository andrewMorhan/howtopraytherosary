The Glorious Mysteries

How to Pray the Rosary (Sunday Order)

Rosary Prayers Reference

Sign of the Cross
In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Apostles' Creed
I believe in God, the Father almighty, Creator of heaven and earth, and in Jesus Christ, His only Son, our Lord, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, died and was buried; He descended into hell; on the third day He rose again from the dead; He ascended into heaven, and is seated at the right hand of God the Father almighty; from there He will come to judge the living and the dead. I believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy Catholic Church, the communion of saints, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection of the body, and life everlasting. Amen.

Our Father
Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be Thy name. Thy kingdom come, Thy will be done, on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread; and forgive us our trespasses, as we forgive those who trespass against us; and lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil. Amen.

Hail Mary
Hail Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with thee. Blessed art thou among women, and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus. Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners, now and at the hour of our death. Amen.

Glory Be
Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit. As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.

Hail, Holy Queen
Hail, Holy Queen, Mother of mercy, our life, our sweetness, and our hope. To thee do we cry, poor banished children of Eve. To thee do we send up our sighs, mourning and weeping in this valley of tears. Turn then, most gracious advocate, thine eyes of mercy toward us, and after this our exile, show unto us the blessed fruit of thy womb, Jesus. O clement, O loving, O sweet Virgin Mary. Pray for us, O Holy Mother of God, that we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ. Amen.

1. The Resurrection

Announce the First Glorious Mystery. Pray 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, and 1 Glory Be.

  • Mary Magdalene discovering the empty tomb
  • Peter and John running to the tomb
  • Jesus appearing to Mary Magdalene
  • Jesus revealing Himself to the disciples
  • Thomas placing his fingers in Jesus’ wounds
  • The disciples’ joy upon seeing the risen Lord
  • The command to preach to all nations
  • The hope of eternal life
  • The power of the resurrection over sin and death
  • Jesus’ victory fulfilling the Scriptures
2. The Ascension

Announce the Second Glorious Mystery. Pray 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, and 1 Glory Be.

  • Jesus leading His disciples to the Mount of Olives
  • Jesus blessing them before ascending
  • The command to baptize in the name of the Trinity
  • The cloud taking Jesus out of their sight
  • The promise of the Holy Spirit
  • The disciples returning to Jerusalem with joy
  • Heaven opening for the risen Christ
  • Jesus seated at the right hand of the Father
  • The angels telling the disciples He will return
  • Hope in Jesus’ second coming
3. The Descent of the Holy Spirit

Announce the Third Glorious Mystery. Pray 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, and 1 Glory Be.

  • The disciples and Mary gathered in the upper room
  • The sound of a mighty rushing wind
  • Tongues of fire resting on each of them
  • They were filled with the Holy Spirit
  • Speaking in different tongues
  • Bold proclamation of the Gospel
  • Peter preaching to the crowd
  • Thousands converting that day
  • The birth of the Church
  • The promise that the Spirit remains with us
4. The Assumption of Mary

Announce the Fourth Glorious Mystery. Pray 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, and 1 Glory Be.

  • Mary’s earthly life coming to an end
  • The apostles gathering at her side
  • Mary falling asleep in peace (the Dormition)
  • Jesus receiving His mother into Heaven
  • Her body preserved from corruption
  • The angels rejoicing at her arrival
  • Mary’s glorified body entering Heaven
  • The Church honoring her assumption
  • Hope for our own resurrection in glory
  • Mary interceding for us in Heaven
5. The Coronation of Mary

Announce the Fifth Glorious Mystery. Pray 1 Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, and 1 Glory Be.

  • Mary welcomed into Heaven by angels and saints
  • Jesus placing a crown upon her head
  • Mary exalted above all creatures
  • The Queen Mother interceding for us
  • The heavenly court rejoicing
  • Mary as model of perfect discipleship
  • Mary shining with glory and grace
  • Jesus honoring His mother eternally
  • Mary reigning beside her Son
  • Hope in our eternal reward in Heaven
Closing Prayers

Hail, Holy Queen
Hail, Holy Queen, Mother of mercy, our life, our sweetness, and our hope. To thee do we cry, poor banished children of Eve. To thee do we send up our sighs, mourning and weeping in this valley of tears. Turn then, most gracious advocate, thine eyes of mercy toward us, and after this our exile, show unto us the blessed fruit of thy womb, Jesus. O clement, O loving, O sweet Virgin Mary. Pray for us, O Holy Mother of God, that we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ. Amen.

Sign of the Cross
In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Scripture References
  • The Resurrection: Matthew 28:1–10, Mark 16:1–8, Luke 24:1–12, John 20:1–18
  • The Ascension: Luke 24:50–53, Acts 1:6–12
  • The Descent of the Holy Spirit: Acts 2:1–13
  • The Assumption of Mary: Revelation 12:1, Psalm 132:8
    (Traditionally upheld by Sacred Tradition — no direct verse describes it)
  • The Coronation of Mary: Revelation 12:1, 1 Kings 2:19
    (Rooted in Scripture and Tradition — Mary as Queen Mother)
